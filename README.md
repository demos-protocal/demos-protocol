# 🔺 Demos Protocol

**A direct democracy currency for Oregon.**  
Your investment is your vote. Built on Polygon. Open source.

---

## What is Demos?

Demos is a blockchain-based civic currency giving every Oregon 
resident an equal stake in their community's future.

Three mechanics. One system:

- **Universal Entry** — every Oregon resident receives one free 
  Demos Token (DMT)
- **Wealth Ceiling** — no wallet can hoard beyond a set limit. 
  Excess circulates back to the community
- **Investment = Vote** — allocating tokens to programs 
  simultaneously funds and votes for them. No separate ballot. 
  No politicians. No middlemen.

---

## Why Demos?

Current democracy runs on election cycles.  
Current markets run on capital accumulation.  
Neither serves ordinary people consistently.

Demos collapses both into one act — when you invest in your 
community, you govern it.

## Who Can Participate?

**Oregon residents** — free token, full voting rights  
**Outside supporters** — purchase DMT to fund community 
investment pools, no voting rights

Capital flows in. Governance stays local.
